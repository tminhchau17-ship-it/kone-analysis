# Methodology Notes

## Project purpose
This dashboard is a financial-analysis case study on KONE Oyj designed for portfolio use. It combines:
- reported historical financial figures,
- derived analytical ratios,
- scenario-based forecast assumptions,
- and a simulated management-style variance view for FY2024.

## Data classification
The dashboard uses four data types:

### 1. Reported
Figures taken from public company disclosures and investor materials.
Examples:
- annual revenue
- EBIT margin
- free cash flow
- segment mix where publicly disclosed

### 2. Derived
Metrics calculated from reported figures.
Examples:
- YoY growth
- revenue CAGR
- cost ratios
- EBIT bridge deltas

### 3. Scenario assumptions
Author-built forward-looking estimates for FY2025–FY2026.
Examples:
- bull / base / bear revenue
- margin path
- EPS and dividend scenarios
- probability weights

### 4. Simulated management view
Illustrative internal-style reporting created for analytical storytelling.
Examples:
- monthly actual vs budget variance
- business-unit variance table
- waterfall from budget EBIT to actual EBIT

## Forecast approach
The forecast section is scenario-based and intended as an analytical case study rather than investment advice.
Inputs include:
- historical trend direction,
- management guidance referenced in public materials,
- business mix resilience from maintenance revenue,
- and macro risk, especially China property exposure.

## Important limitation
The monthly P&L variance and business-unit monthly budget views are not presented as official internal KONE reporting. They are a portfolio-case simulation built to demonstrate FP&A-style analysis.
